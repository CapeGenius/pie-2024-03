Electrical

The electrical work and features in the Portal Turret replica.

The circuit contains various sensors to respond to external stimulus and motors to control movement.

Circuit Diagram

The circuit contains the following components: an accelerometer/magnetometer, a sound sensing module, an IR receiver, a PCF8591 ADC/DAC module, a button, a 9V DC motor, a L293D motor driver, and two 9g SG90 servos. Additionally, a speaker is connected to the Raspberry Pi via Bluetooth and an IR remote control can be used to send IR codes to the IR receiver.
