AABC PORTAL TURRET

A replica of the turret from the video game Portal.

Objectives

Our goal was to create a replica of the Turret from the video game Portal. We wanted it to be able to move according to external stimulus via motors and sensors, play music and sounds, and have different modes that it could cycle through.

Mechanical

Giving the Turret a means of opening and closing the wings, spinning them around, and being able to hold itself up with legs.

Electrical

Featuring a Raspberry Pi 4 Model B, sound sensor, LSM303AGR, PCF8591, L293D motor driver, IR receiver, DC motors, LEDs, and servos.

Software

Features multiple modes, including manual, music playing, and stimulus detection mode.
